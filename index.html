OpenCV IOS App

Computer Vision Engineer

Tasked by CEO to create a computer vision App that would take an image and distort\scale it such that it could be used for manufacturing. Read research papers and many openCV forms to get ideas as to what to do. Utilize the React Native and JavaScript in tandem with OpenCV to create a desktop application. One of the biggest challenges was to negate the effects of lens distortion even after applying correction matrices to the images.

The current system allows a technician to take a photo using a smart device and upload it to the cloud. The photo is made immediately available for manufacturing from a projected image. This system has reduced lead times for out of state purchases by two weeks, while reducing cost, and adding $70,000 (USD) of value to the company's market. At this point in time Christopher's algorithm has processed 20,422 images on the cloud and counting.
